Strawberry Milkshake

Description

A strawberry milkshake is a yummy drink that can be made with strawberry flavouring or real strawberries

Added ice cream for taste and thickness is optional too

Ingredients

  • 200g fresh strawberries, hulled
  • 300ml cold milk
  • 3 scoops of vanilla or strawberry ice cream

Steps

  1. Add the strawberries, milk and ice cream to a food processor and blend until smooth
  2. Pour into a glass of your choosing and enjoy!
