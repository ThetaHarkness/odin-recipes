Chocolate Mug Cake

Description

A melt in your mouth mug cake that takes seconds to heat up in the microwave

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of sunflower oil
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons of plain flour
  • 3 tablespoons of light brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of cocoa powder
  • half a teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon boiling water
  • 1 soft chocolate truffle of your choosing

Steps

  1. Whisk all the ingredients except for the soft chocolate truffle with a pinch of salt in a large mug
  2. Push the chocolate into the centre of the mixture and microwave on high heat for 45 seconds, or until cooked on the outside with a smooth inside
