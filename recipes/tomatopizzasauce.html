Tomato Pizza Sauce

Description

A thick tomato sauce to add to a pizza base

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 400g can of chopped tomatoes
  • 3 tablespoons of tomato puree
  • 1 small bunch of basil, finely chopped

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a saucepan over a low heat
  2. Add the tomatoes, the puree and bring to the boil, lowering the heat
  3. Simmer until thickened and reduced then stir the basil into the sauce
  4. The sauce will cover 4-6 large pizza bases, it will keep in the fridge for one week
